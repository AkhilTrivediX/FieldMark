# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Small business operators, bookkeepers, and accountants who repeatedly process invoices, receipts, bank statements, delivery challans, and tax documents. They are usually focused, time-constrained, and cautious because mistakes become accounting cleanup later.

## Product Purpose

Fieldmark converts business documents into validated, evidence-linked structured data while keeping files local by default. Success means users trust the extracted JSON because each field has visible source evidence and every important number passes deterministic checks before export.

## Brand Personality

Precise, private, workmanlike. The product should feel like a clean document bench: quiet enough for daily work, but distinctive through its evidence marks, validation rails, and local-vault posture.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic SaaS dashboards, icon-card grids, gradient hero sections, decorative blob backgrounds, glassmorphism, oversized marketing metrics, and "AI scanner" novelty styling. Avoid looking like a generic expense app or a PDF utility clone with brighter colors.

## Design Principles

- Make the document the center of gravity.
- Show proof beside every extracted value.
- Let validation, not decoration, create visual drama.
- Keep local/cloud behavior explicit and impossible to misunderstand.
- Prefer dense, calm working surfaces over marketing polish.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA minimum. Validation state must never rely on color alone. Motion should be functional and respect reduced-motion preferences. Layout must support long vendor names, large currency values, and lower-quality scans without text overlap.
