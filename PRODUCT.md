# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Small business operators, bookkeepers, accountants, and finance teams who process invoices, receipts, statements, delivery documents, and tax records every day. They are usually working through batches, under time pressure, and with a low tolerance for silent extraction mistakes.

## Product Purpose

FieldMark turns business documents into validated, evidence-linked structured records while keeping documents local by default. It should reduce manual invoice entry, show exactly where each extracted value came from, flag bad source scans before approval, and block exports when deterministic checks fail.

Success means a user can import a messy document, review fields against source evidence, correct mistakes, trust the calculated totals, and export JSON or CSV without sending private documents to a human reviewer.

## Brand Personality

Precise, private, workmanlike.

The product should feel like a clean document bench: calm enough for daily use, but distinctive through evidence marks, validation rails, scan-quality checks, and explicit local-vault behavior.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic SaaS dashboards, gradient hero pages, decorative blob backgrounds, glassmorphism, giant marketing metrics, cartoon AI scanner styling, and shallow PDF utility clones. The interface should not feel like a generic expense app, a toy OCR demo, or a bright template dashboard wearing invoice labels.

## Design Principles

- Make the document the center of gravity.
- Show proof beside every extracted value.
- Let validation and scan quality create visual priority.
- Keep local/cloud behavior explicit and impossible to misunderstand.
- Prefer dense, calm working surfaces over marketing decoration.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA minimum. Validation state must never rely on color alone. Keyboard focus must be visible. Motion must be functional and respect reduced-motion preferences. Layout must support long vendor names, large currency values, narrow screens, and low-quality scans without text overlap.
